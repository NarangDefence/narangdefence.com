About Narang Defence

Counsel who has worked both sides of the courtroom.

Tarun Narang founded Narang Defence after acting as a standing agent for the Department of Justice on s. 490 applications for the RCMP in the Okanagan. Earlier, he articled in Prince George, British Columbia, where he had sole conduct of multiple contested matters in communities across the Northern Interior.

That perspective — knowing how the Crown approaches its file — informs every defence Narang Defence builds today.

Our approach

Three principles that shape every file.

Strategic from day one

Every file starts with a careful review of the disclosure, the Charter landscape, and the Crown's theory — so the defence is built on what the case actually requires, not a template.

Plainly explained

Criminal procedure is full of moving parts. Clients are walked through the process, the realistic options, and the consequences of each — in plain language, before any decision is made.

Direct access

Narang Defence is a sole practice. The lawyer who reviews your file is the lawyer who appears in court, takes your calls, and answers your questions.
